Where does Live Ops monitor?

Live Ops primarily watches the stream at the AMPP stage — bitrates, job health, and source stability. That's where most interventions happen.

🔁

What is a backup source?

If the primary source fails, a backup encoder (often at a different location) can be switched in to keep the stream alive with minimal viewer impact.

📉

What causes bitrate drops?

Network congestion, encoder issues, and hardware failures can all cause the bitrate to drop. A sustained drop usually means something is wrong upstream.

⚡

How fast does Live Ops need to react?

Most SLAs require acknowledgment within 1–2 minutes of an issue. Delays let problems propagate to viewers and are harder to recover from.
